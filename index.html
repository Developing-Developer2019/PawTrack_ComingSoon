Privacy Policy – PawTrack

Effective Date: June 2025

1. Introduction
PawTrack ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and store personal data in line with UK GDPR regulations.

2. What We Collect

  • Basic Analytics (No Consent Required): We collect anonymous, aggregated data about website usage including page views, session duration, and general geographic regions. This data cannot be used to identify individual users and is GDPR compliant without explicit consent.
  • Enhanced Analytics (Requires Consent): With your consent, we enable additional analytics features that may include user journey tracking and more detailed session information. All data remains anonymized.
  • Email Addresses: When you sign up for early access, we securely store your email to notify you when the platform becomes available.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To understand website performance and user experience through anonymous analytics
  • To send one-time updates regarding early access availability
  • Enhanced analytics (only with consent) helps us improve specific user flows and features

4. Data Retention
Your email will only be retained until early access invitations are complete, after which you can request removal at any time.

5. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Google Analytics is used solely for internal insights.

6. Your Rights
Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. To do so, contact us at privacy@pawtrack.uk.

7. Contact
For any privacy-related questions, email us at privacy@pawtrack.uk.
